While major Florida cities like Miami and Tampa have median home prices exceeding $500,000, affordable housing exists in other regions. There are still parts of Florida where you can buy a home for under $100,000 — and in some cases, live near the Gulf Coast with a monthly mortgage payment below $600. ## 10 Cheapest Places to Buy a House in Florida (2026)

### Lafayette County

**Median Home Price:** $129,350 | **Monthly Payment:** $740

Small, rural north-central county with 8,000 residents. County seat is Mayo. Rural counties like Lafayette may qualify for [USDA loans with zero down payment](/learn/usda-loans-florida).

### Hardee County

**Median Home Price:** $127,190 | **Monthly Payment:** $720

Central Florida location with county seat in Wauchula. Historically significant phosphate mining region.

### Liberty County

**Median Home Price:** $115,590 | **Monthly Payment:** $660

Florida's least populous county in the Big Bend region. Bristol is the county seat.

### Glades County

**Median Home Price:** $112,840 | **Monthly Payment:** $640

Located in the Florida Heartland. Moore Haven is the county seat on Lake Okeechobee.

### Jackson County

**Median Home Price:** $111,950 | **Monthly Payment:** $640

Panhandle county established in 1822. Marianna is the county seat.

### Hamilton County

**Median Home Price:** $108,510 | **Monthly Payment:** $620

North Central Florida established in 1827. Jasper is the county seat. The Suwannee River forms a natural boundary.

### Madison County

**Median Home Price:** $104,090 | **Monthly Payment:** $590

North-central region. Madison is the county seat, named after the fourth U.S. president.

### Holmes County

**Median Home Price:** $99,660 | **Monthly Payment:** $570

Panhandle county. Bonifay is the county seat.

### Taylor County

**Median Home Price:** $98,480 | **Monthly Payment:** $560

Big Bend location. Perry is the county seat, known as the "Tree Capital of the South."

### Dixie County

**Median Home Price:** $95,240 | **Monthly Payment:** $540

Big Bend and Gulf Coast region. Cross City is the county seat. The Lower Suwannee National Wildlife Refuge provides outdoor opportunities.

## Cheapest Places to Live Near Miami, Florida

**Closest/Most Affordable Balance:** Broward County with approximately $476,900 median and $2,720 average rent.

**More Affordable Alternatives:** Hendry County and Okeechobee County, both under $190,000 median with approximately $1,000 rent.

**Most Budget-Friendly:** Glades County at $112,840 median with $640 estimated payment (more rural).

## Cheapest Places to Live Near Jacksonville, Florida

**Central Location:** Duval County (Jacksonville) with approximately $332,480 median and $1,910 rent.

**Nearby Affordable Options:** Clay County at $349,650 and Nassau County at $425,680.

**More Budget-Friendly:** Putnam County at $157,460 median with $960 rent, and Baker County at $232,750 with $1,350 rent.

## Cheapest Places to Live Near Tampa, Florida

**Central Location:** Hillsborough County (Tampa) with approximately $408,390 median and $2,330 rent.

**Closer Affordable Options:** Polk County at $309,760 and Pasco County at $348,090.

**More Budget-Friendly:** Hernando County at $314,480 and Citrus County at $274,030 with $1,680 rent. If you're not ready to buy, consider [rent-to-own programs in Florida](/learn/florida-rent-to-own-programs) as a path to ownership.

## Cheapest Places to Live Near The Ocean

**Most Affordable Coastal:** Dixie County at $95,240 median with $540 payment, and Taylor County at $98,480 with $560 payment.

**Moderately Affordable Coastal:** Franklin County at $249,950 and Levy County at $168,470.

**Coastal with More Amenities:** Bay County at $331,150 and Citrus County at $274,030.

## Affordable Florida Homeownership Is Still Possible

Despite Florida's luxury reputation, homeownership remains achievable. Combining lower prices with [grants and programs for Florida first-time buyers](/learn/first-time-homebuyer/grants-and-programs), [first-time homebuyer loan programs](/first-time-home-buyer), FHA financing, or zero-down options makes ownership even more achievable. Most buyers qualify for at least one assistance program — review the [requirements to buy a house in Florida](/learn/requirements-to-buy-a-house-in-florida) to get started.
