Purchasing a home in Florida as a self-employed or 1099 worker does not require flawless tax documentation or traditional W-2 employment status.

In 2026, Florida provides several pathways including bank-statement loans, 1099-exclusive mortgages, rent-to-own arrangements, earned-equity programs, and [down payment assistance](/learn/first-time-homebuyer/grants-and-programs) tailored for entrepreneurs, freelancers, gig workers, and small-business owners.

## 11 Best Mortgage Programs for Self-Employed Florida Buyers

### Bank Statement Mortgage

**Type:** Non-QM Mortgage | **Best for:** Self-employed buyers with strong deposits but low taxable income

Lenders examine 12 to 24 months of personal or business bank statements rather than tax returns. Monthly deposits are averaged, with 50% to 100% potentially counted as qualifying income.

**Typical requirements:** 620 to 680+ credit score, 10 to 20% down payment, 1 to 2 years self-employment, cash reserves required.

### 1099-Only Mortgage

**Type:** Non-QM Mortgage | **Best for:** Contractors, freelancers, gig workers

Uses 1 to 2 years of 1099 income without requiring tax returns or W-2s. Some lenders allow 90% to 100% of gross income.

**Requirements:** 620+ credit, 10 to 20% down, 12 to 24 months of 1099s.

### Florida Hometown Heroes

**Type:** Down payment + reduced-rate first mortgage | **Best for:** Full-time self-employed Floridians

Up to 5% of loan amount (maximum $35,000) as a 0% deferred second mortgage with discounted [FHA](/learn/fha-loan-eligibility-requirements-florida), VA, USDA, or conventional rate.

**Key requirements:** 640+ credit, full-time work (35+ hours/week), [income and purchase-price limits](/learn/florida-housing-income-purchase-price-limits), homebuyer education completion.

### FHA Earned Equity Program

**Type:** Government-backed earned-equity path | **Best for:** Buyers who can manage payments but don't yet qualify

A nonprofit or tribal entity acquires the home using an FHA loan. The buyer occupies the property under a long-term lease or ownership agreement while building equity and preparing to refinance or assume the loan later.

### Dream America (Rent-to-Own)

**Type:** Lease-purchase | **Best for:** Buyers approaching mortgage readiness

**Key requirements:** 12-month lease term, 500+ credit, $8,000+ in savings.

### Divvy Homes (Rent-to-Own)

**Type:** Rent-to-own arrangement | **Best for:** Those with variable income or limited savings

1 to 2% upfront cost with a portion of rent building purchase credit. Available in major Florida metropolitan areas.

### TRIO Lease-to-Own (FHA Assumable)

**Type:** Structured lease-to-own | **Best for:** Moderate-income self-employed buyers

FHA loan assumed later. 580+ credit required with stable income necessary.

### One-Year Self-Employment Mortgage

**Type:** Freddie Mac or Non-QM | **Best for:** Recently self-employed professionals

One year self-employment history with prior W-2 experience in the same field. Strong reserves required.

### Freddie Mac Home Possible

**Type:** 3%-down conventional loan | **Best for:** Lower-income self-employed buyers

660+ credit required with income limits applying. Allows gifts, grants, and co-borrowers.

### Asset Depletion Mortgage

**Type:** Portfolio / Non-QM | **Best for:** High-net-worth buyers

Assets substitute for income documentation. $500k+ liquid assets required with large down payment typical.

### Co-Borrower Strategy (FHA or Conventional)

**Type:** FHA or conventional loan | **Best for:** Buyers with family support available

Co-borrower income strengthens qualification. Available nationwide.

## Bottom Line

Self-employed and 1099 workers need not possess impeccable tax documentation to purchase a home in Florida during 2026.

With the appropriate program — whether bank statements, 1099 income, earned equity, or assistance — qualification becomes achievable based on actual cash flow rather than tax-return calculations.
