The [Florida Hometown Heroes Program](/hometown-heroes) serves as a statewide homebuyer assistance initiative designed for first-time purchasers working in essential Florida-based professions. Key benefits include:

- Up to $35,000 in down payment and closing cost assistance
- Assistance structured as a 0% interest, deferred second mortgage
- Repayment only required upon sale, refinance, or loss of primary residence
- Reduced mortgage rates
- No origination points from participating lenders
- Compatibility with FHA, VA, USDA, and conventional loan options (program-specific)

## How Job Eligibility Works

To meet occupational requirements, at least one borrower must:

- Work full-time (verified by verification of employment and paystubs)
- Be employed by a Florida-based, brick-and-mortar employer
- Hold a position listed in an approved occupational category

## Florida Hometown Heroes Job Eligibility Checklist (By Profession)

### Healthcare Workers

Eligible positions include those working full-time for Florida-based healthcare providers, physicians, hospitals, assisted living facilities, mental health facilities, or healthcare institutions.

**Nursing:** Advanced Practice Registered Nurse (APRN), Clinical Nurse Specialist, Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN), Nurse Anesthetist, Nurse Midwife, Nursing Assistant (CNA), Nursing Technician, Registered Nurse (RN). Nurses may also qualify for the [Florida Nurse Next Door Program](/learn/florida-nurse-next-door-program).

**Medical Technicians:** Anesthesia Support Technician, Cardiology Technician, Clinical Technician, CT Technician, ER Technician, Laboratory Technician (Including Phlebotomist), Music Technician, Neurology Technician, Nuclear Medicine Technician, Monitor Technician, Orthopedic Technician, Pharmacy Technician, Radiation Technician, Rehabilitation Technician, Sonographer, Surgical Technicians, X-ray Technician

**Medical Therapists:** Audiology Therapist, Occupational Therapist, Physical Therapist, Respiratory Therapist, Speech Therapist

**Mental Health Workers:** Behavioral Health Specialist, Behavioral Health Technician, Behavioral Health Therapist, Case Manager, Mental Health Specialist, Mental Health Technician, Mental Health Therapist, Psychiatrist, Psychologist, Social Worker

**Doctors, Physicians, and Pharmacists:** Anesthesiologist, Anesthesiologist Assistant, Dentist, Nuclear Pharmacist, Pharmacist, Pharmacy Technician, Physician, Physician Assistant

**Other Healthcare Workers:** Dental Assistant, Dental Hygienist, Medical Assistant (CMA or MA), Patient Care Assistant, Patient Transporter (Orderly), Phlebotomist

### School Staff Members

Full-time employees of Florida-based K-12 public, private, charter, or magnet schools qualify, including:

- Assistant Principal
- Career Specialist (Guidance Counselor)
- Classroom Teacher
- Food Service / Cafeteria Staff
- Librarian/Media Specialist
- Maintenance / Janitorial Staff
- Principal
- School Counselor
- School Psychologist
- Social Worker
- Teacher Aide

### First Responders

Full-time employees of Florida-based fire departments, ambulance services, or hospitals qualify, including:

- Certified Emergency Medical Technician (EMT)
- Certified Paramedic
- Firefighter

First responders may also be eligible for additional [first responder grants and programs](/learn/florida-first-responder-homebuyer-grants-programs).

### Public Safety Workers

Full-time employees of Florida-based local, county, state, or federal law enforcement departments, juvenile detention facilities, correctional facilities, or emergency operations centers qualify, including:

- 911 Public Safety Communicator (911 Operator or Dispatcher)
- Correctional Officer
- Correctional Probation Officer
- Juvenile Detention Officer
- Juvenile Probation Officer
- Sworn Law Enforcement Officer (Local, State, or Federal)

### Court Employees

Full-time employees of Florida-based county, state, or federal courts, court administrator offices, State Attorney Offices, or Public Defender Offices qualify, including:

- Assistant State Attorney
- Assistant Public Defender
- Attorney (if employed by Florida Supreme Court, District Courts of Appeal, Circuit Courts, or county courts)
- Court Clerk
- Court Stenographer/Court Reporter (if employed by Florida Supreme Court, District Courts of Appeal, Circuit Courts, or county courts)

### Childcare Workers

Full-time employees of Florida-based licensed child care facilities pursuant to state statutes or those exempt from licensure qualify:

- Any worker at a licensed child care facility
- Any worker at a licensed or registered home day care

### Military and Veterans

Eligible applicants include:

- All active duty or reserve service members of all branches of the United States Armed Forces, including the Florida National Guard
- Veterans employed full-time with a Florida-based employer who have been discharged under other than dishonorable conditions from eligible military or reserve branches

## Additional Eligibility Requirements (All Applicants)

Beyond occupational qualifications, applicants must also:

- Be a first-time homebuyer (or not have owned in the past 3 years)
- Meet county [Hometown Heroes income limits](/learn/florida-hometown-heroes-income-limits)
- Purchase a primary residence in Florida
- Complete homebuyer education
- Use an [approved Hometown Heroes lender](/learn/florida-hometown-heroes-approved-lenders)

The Florida Hometown Heroes Program remains among the most impactful homebuyer assistance initiatives available in Florida for 2026, particularly benefiting essential service professionals.

While occupational eligibility serves as an initial requirement, additional factors also apply.
